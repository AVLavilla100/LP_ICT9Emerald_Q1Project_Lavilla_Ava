Gummy Eggs

Gummy eggs are soft, chewy candies shaped like sunny-side-up fried eggs. They typically have a white, gummy base with a yellow "yolk" in the center, made from fruit-flavored gelatin. While they resemble real fried eggs in appearance, they taste sweet, with fruity or citrus flavors. Gummy eggs are a popular novelty candy, often included in mixed candy assortments or themed confectionery sets. Their playful design and chewy texture make them a fun treat for candy lovers of all ages.
